Myself

Hi, my name is Therrense Lua. I first got into programming straight out of high school. Took a gap year before heading to Uni and joined a media company to be their iOS Developer Intern. I knew I had found ‘my thing’ when I could keep going for hours on end. I’d rock up to work every day at 10am (everyone’s starting time) and I would leave at 10pm. 12-hour work days flew by because I loved what I was doing. This brings me to now, a recent graduate from The University of Melbourne majored in Computing & Software Systems. In my spare time, I work on projects close to my heart and have helped organise a few hackathons (Unihack). Oh, and I also love powerlifting.
